NamDriver Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Summary: NamDriver connects car owners with drivers in Namibia. We collect only the data necessary to operate this service. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third-party analytics providers.

1. Who We Are

NamDriver is operated by NamStack ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the NamDriver mobile application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Account type (car owner, driver, or admin)

2.2 Profile Information

Depending on your account type, you may provide:

  • Profile photo
  • Location (city/town in Namibia)
  • Bio/description
  • Languages spoken

2.3 Driver-Specific Information

If you register as a driver, we additionally collect:

  • Years of driving experience
  • Vehicle types you can operate
  • Availability (full-time, part-time, weekends only)
  • Professional Driving Permit (PDP) status
  • Expected salary range
  • Preferred work areas
  • Work history (employer names, positions, dates, references)

2.4 Verification Documents

For driver verification, we may collect copies of:

  • Driver's license
  • Professional Driving Permit (PDP)
  • National ID document
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Reference letters
Sensitive Data: Identity documents are stored in private, encrypted storage. Only you and authorized administrators can access your documents.

2.5 User-Generated Content

Through your use of the App, we collect:

  • Chat messages between car owners and drivers
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Job postings and job interest submissions
  • Reports submitted about other users

2.6 Automatically Collected Data

  • Profile view counts
  • Notification delivery records
  • Account activity timestamps (last login, profile updates)

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Account creation and authentication Name, email, phone, password
Displaying driver profiles to car owners Profile info, experience, ratings, availability
Verifying driver credentials Identity documents, license, PDP
Facilitating communication Chat messages, notifications
Managing job postings Job details, interest submissions
Trust and safety Reviews, ratings, user reports

4. Data Sharing

4.1 With Other Users

Certain information is visible to other users of the App as part of its core functionality:

  • Driver profiles (name, photo, experience, ratings, availability) are visible to car owners
  • Car owner names are visible to drivers they interact with
  • Reviews are publicly visible on driver profiles

4.2 Service Providers

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider for database hosting, user authentication, file storage, and real-time messaging. Supabase processes your data on our behalf and is contractually obligated to protect it.

4.3 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal data to any third party
  • Share your data with advertising networks
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • Share your data with data brokers
  • Track you across other apps or websites

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure. We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Verification documents are stored in private storage buckets with access controls
  • Row-level security policies ensure users can only access authorized data
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

6. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Chat messages are retained for the duration of the conversation
  • Reviews remain visible unless the reviewer or reviewed driver deletes their account
  • When you delete your account, all associated data (profile, documents, messages, reviews, engagements) is permanently deleted

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile settings
  • Update your information at any time via the App
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your personal data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent for data processing by deleting your account

8. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of documents for verification
  • Photo Library: To upload profile photos and documents

These permissions are optional and requested only when needed. The App does not access your camera or photos without your explicit consent.

9. Children's Privacy

NamDriver is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.
